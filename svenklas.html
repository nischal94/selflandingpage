Svenklas

Victory for the Youth

Svenklas—meaning "Victory for the Youth" in Norse—is a design-driven sustainable luxury lifestyle and travel accessory brand creating minimal, functional, and aesthetically appealing products using innovative premium natural materials for life's everyday journeys.

Founding Story

In 2019, Nischal Sharma and Nikhil Sharma founded Svenklas with an initial investment of Rs 5 lakh. The duo met in Gurugram in 2017 while working on a design project at a marketing firm called Ebee Global. United by their shared vision for sustainable design, they launched the brand to develop thoughtful products using plant-based natural materials.

Products

The Gurugram-based brand offers a carefully curated range of premium accessories including backpacks, messenger bags, tote bags, hip packs, and wallets. While the brand recently expanded into T-shirts, bags remain the core focus.

Each product is designed with meticulous attention to detail, balancing form and function while prioritizing sustainable materials and ethical production methods.

Sustainability Commitment

Sustainability lies at the heart of Svenklas. The brand has partnered with GiveIndia to plant two moringa trees for every product sold, actively contributing to reforestation efforts.

The Svenklas Recycle Shop enables customers to return products after use for proper recycling, closing the loop on product lifecycles and minimizing environmental impact.

Market Position

Currently serving over 5,000 customers across India with almost 6,500 completed orders, Svenklas targets millennials and travelers aged 23-35 with good disposable income. The brand's average order value stands at Rs 4,000.

Svenklas has garnered recognition from leading publications including Grazia India, YourStory, Man's World, Little Black Book, Fashion Network, Times Internet, and Homegrown.
